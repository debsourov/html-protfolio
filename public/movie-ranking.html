The Best Movies According to Sourov

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Interstellar

This is my favorite Sci fi movie about time travel. Directed by Christopher Nolan a masterpiece, full of wonder and amazement.

Forgotten

One of the best South Korean psychological thriller movie that will keep viewers guessing throughout, with tension and mystery that serve as a masterclass for executing dark and twisted storylines.

Avengers: Endgame

As I am a great fan of Marvel and I love action & Superheros movies. Watching all our favorite superheroes in one film is just surpassing.
